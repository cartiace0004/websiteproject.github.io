MTN Nigeria Customer Churn Exploratory Data Analysis

MTN Nigeria Communications PLC is the leading telecommunications provider in Nigeria and a subsidiary of the MTN Group, a multinational telecom company headquartered in South Africa. Established in 2000, MTN Nigeria has grown to become the country's largest mobile network operator per Emis.

Customer churn is a business phenomenon where customers stop doing business with a company or stop using a product/service over a given period of time. Also known as customer attrition.

This analysis of customer churn for MTN Nigeria is of critical importance given the company's extensive subscriber base and diverse service offerings. By investigating why customers choose to discontinue their services, this project aims to provide actionable insights that directly support key business objectives. The findings will be instrumental in developing more effective customer retention strategies, highlighting specific areas for service improvement, and informing a more precise and targeted market strategy based on patterns observed across different demographics and regions. Ultimately, understanding the dynamics of customer churn will enable MTN to enhance its service quality and strengthen its market position.
