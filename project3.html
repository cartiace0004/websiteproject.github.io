E-Commerce Order and Supply Chain Exploratory Data Analysis

This project analyzes E-Commerce orders and payment data to uncover and provide insights on sales performance, customer behavior and order fulfillments.

The primary objective of this project is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of key e-commerce operations to uncover actionable insights. We will begin with a Sales Performance Analysis, examining total sales, top-selling products, and revenue trends over time, while also evaluating month-over-month growth and sales seasonality. Following this, we will perform a Customer Behavior Analysis to understand how sales are influenced by a customer's region, their preferred payment methods, and the average amount spent per transaction. Lastly, the project will investigate Order Fulfillment Efficiency by analyzing the rates of early versus late deliveries and identifying any regions that experience consistent delays.
